Introduction



Built On Trust


Prabhakunj came into being during the year 2011, in a small but very progressive town, Navsari located at the Southern State of Gujarat in India. Its existence was incorporated with a vision to bring about a change through the overall development of society at large.

It’s a known fact that the overall progress of any society is only possible when all the sections of the society are Uplifted, Educated, Employed and been provided good Health Care facilities. The upper strata of the society can easily afford the said things, also they are very much aware about the importance of the same; so they are definitely going to do their bit and work towards their individual progress. But this section is just 20% of the entire population. The other 80% which falls under the category of Lower Middle and Middle Class strata either don’t have the resources to work towards their own development or they are not even aware about the need and importance of the same.

It’s time someone’s got to work for uplifting the said section of population so as to contribute towards the overall development of the entire society; we just cannot let the vulnerability and inequality go unnoticed, either by our choices or preferences.

Prabhakunj works under that philosophy. Its working for the betterment of this particular segment of the society which needs some support so as to grow and live a Sustainable, Affordable and Decent life, which is not a luxury but a basic necessity for any human.

Prabhakunj is a Parent Concern and has various Sister Concerns under its head, namely

A. Prabhakunj Realty

B. Prabhakunj Pharmaceuticals Pvt. Ltd.

C. Prabhakunj Foundation By Jiral Patel

We are spreading our wings through other Ventures in the near future, namely

D. Prabhakunj Hospitality

E. Prabhakunj Information Technology Pvt. Ltd.

F. Prabhakunj Education

We are a growing and a learning company, so we do realize we still need to go a long way in achieving our ultimate vision. But as they say; with small steps, one can achieve the farthest of the goals.
